FROM THE CAPTAIN

The lanthorn.

I tried every vision board app I could find. One sent me notifications about finding love. The app had decided, without asking, what I should want.

Another had branding that looked stale from the 2010s. I did find one I liked, but when I switched phones I lost all my work. So I built my own.

During the age of sail, a lanthorn was a vital tool. It had to be tended. Letting it go out at night was taboo, viewed as a harbinger of doom.

That's Starboard. The lanthorn you carry. The vision kept visible because you chose to keep it alight, daily, on purpose.

Welcome aboard, Sailor.

— Megan
