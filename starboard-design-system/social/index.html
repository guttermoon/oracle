DID YOU KNOW

The word lanthorn comes from a 16th-century lantern made of translucent animal horn.

Sailors called them lanthorns. Letting one go out at night was a harbinger of doom.

— Maritime lore · vol. 01
CAPTION: A vision board kept visible is a lanthorn kept lit. The flame that points your ship away from the rocks. #maritime #lore #starboardmanifest

The reticular activating system filters 99% of what your eyes see.

Tell it what to care about, and it will find that thing everywhere. This is what a vision board does.
