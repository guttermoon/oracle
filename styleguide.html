# Starboard — "Warm Minimalist"

## Overview
Warm, approachable minimalism with a sophisticated edge. A cream background that
is easier on the eyes than pure white. Calm and focused, low density, generous
whitespace, single-column centered layout. Spacing uses a 4px base unit with
common values at 8, 12, 16, 24, 32. Scale: 0.5, 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 32.

## Colors (semantic tokens)
- background  #f5e6cd  warm cream page background
- surface     #e5e7eb  secondary surfaces and containers
- accent-primary #a4e636  progress, interactive, call-to-action
- accent-light   #faf9d4  subtle accent backgrounds
- text-primary   #1b1f3b  primary text, high contrast
- text-secondary #2d3268  secondary text, medium contrast
- text-muted     #4d5198  supporting content
- pure-black     #000000  logo / emphasis
- pure-white     #ffffff  high-contrast white

Light theme maps background to cream; the dark theme remaps background to deep
navy (#1b1f3b) and text to cream (#f5e6cd) with the same lime accent.

## Typography
- Headline: Playfair Display (serif)
- Body and labels: Chivo (sans-serif)
- 400 regular, 600 to 700 emphasis; tracking grows as text shrinks (0.9px at 9px).
- Large jump from body (16px) to headline (48px) for strong hierarchy.

## Elevation
- Resting: rgba(0,0,0,.4) 0 2px 8px -2px, rgba(0,0,0,.3) 0 1px 2px -1px
- Raised:  rgba(0,0,0,.5) 0 25px 50px -12px

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do use the warm cream background as the primary light surface.
- Don't use pure white as the primary light surface.
- Do keep a 12px radius across interactive elements.
- Don't mix off-scale radius values; stick to 4, 8, 12, 16.
- Do use Playfair sparingly for headlines and brand.
- Don't overuse lime; reserve it for key actions and progress.
- Do keep generous whitespace on the 4px scale.
- Don't crowd elements.
