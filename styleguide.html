Starboard

"Warm Minimalist"

A warm, minimalist design system: elegant serif headings, clean sans-serif body, a single bright accent per theme, generous whitespace, and soft elevation. Three themes share one set of semantic tokens. starboardmanifest.com

Typography

Playfair Display (italic) for headings and brand; Chivo for body, labels, and UI. Amita and Space Grotesk are available as accent faces.

Buttons

One unified system. Variants: primary, secondary, tertiary, ghost, danger. Sizes sm / md / lg. Modifiers: pill, icon.

Pills & tabs (segmented control)

The pill tab bar used for Templates / Catalog, Profile / Billing / Settings, and theme switching. Active segment uses the button fill; the container is a 64px pill.

Cards

Shared elevation model: subtle resting shadow, lift on hover. Surface is card-bg-glass per theme.
